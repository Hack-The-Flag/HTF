About Hack The Flag Competition

Hack The Flag is coming!

Hack the Flag is a competition for anyone who likes to solve ciphers. The competition aims to promote the world of cryptography and encourage people to learn more about it.

The competition will test your skills in solving ciphers and how quickly you can do it. It will start on November 1st, 2023 at 17:00 (UTC+1), and will run for three weeks.

Your goal is to solve as many ciphers as possible and earn as many points as you can. The output of each cipher will be a flag, which you will submit to our website. The flag will look like this: <HTF-this_is_flag/>

If you would like to verify our messages, you can find the key at hacktheflag.net/pgp.

More About Hack The Flag

Our Mission

Hack The Flag is dedicated to promoting cryptography education and awareness through engaging cipher-solving competitions. We believe in making cryptography accessible to everyone, from beginners to experts.

Our platform provides a space for cryptography enthusiasts to test their skills, learn new techniques, and connect with like-minded individuals passionate about codebreaking and security.

Competitions

Throughout the year, Hack The Flag hosts various cryptography competitions, including:

  • Xmas Hunt (December)
  • Seasonal cipher challenges
  • Special themed events
  • Beginner-friendly introductory competitions

Each competition features unique ciphers and puzzles designed to challenge participants at all skill levels.

Skills You will Develop

Participating in Hack The Flag competitions helps you develop valuable skills in:

  • Classical cryptography techniques
  • Modern encryption methods
  • Steganography and hidden messages
  • Logical reasoning and problem-solving
  • Pattern recognition
  • Digital forensics basics
