<p>
Hack the flag is competition for anyone, who like to solve cyphers.
</p>
<p>
We find it important for everyone to find about the world of cryptography, and to learn more about it.
</p>
<p>
This competition will test your skills, how good you are at solving cyphers and how fast can you do it.
</p>
<p>
It will start on 1st November 2023, and will be held for next 3 weeks.
</p>
<p>
Your goal is to solve as many cyphers as you can, and to get as many points as you can.
Output of the cypher will be flag, which you will submit to our website.
